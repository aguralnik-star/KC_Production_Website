# Testimonial Request Template

Use this template when requesting customer testimonial approval. Customize via the admin Request Builder at `/admin/testimonials`.

---

## Default Subject

Request for Testimonial Approval - K&C Design and Manufacturing

---

## Default Email Body

Hi {customer_name},

Thank you for working with K&C Design and Manufacturing.

We are updating our website and would appreciate your permission to use a short testimonial about your experience with K&C.

If you are comfortable providing a testimonial, you can reply with a short statement about your experience related to quality, communication, responsiveness, machining support, tooling, fixtures, gauges, or overall service.

We will not publish your name, company name, project details, photos, or quote unless you approve the final wording and display format.

Possible display options:

- Anonymous testimonial
- First name only
- Role/title only
- Company name only
- Name and company, if approved

Thank you,

K&C Design and Manufacturing  
422 S. Irmen Drive  
Addison, IL 60101  
Phone: (630) 543-3386  
Email: info@kcdesignmfg.com

---

## Anonymous Option Language

Include this when the customer may prefer anonymity:

> We can publish your testimonial anonymously as "Approved Customer" without showing your name or company if you prefer.

---

## Customer Approval Language

When the customer replies, confirm:

1. The exact quote wording they approve
2. Which display option they authorize
3. Where the testimonial may be used (website, proposals, sales materials, social media)
4. The date and method of approval (email, signed form, verbal with follow-up email)

Record all of this in the admin testimonial editor before publishing.

---

## Manual Send Only

This system generates and copies the request — it does **not** send email automatically. Send from your authorized business email and log the request status in admin.
