# Testimonial Capture Workflow

**Project:** K&C Design and Manufacturing Website

---

## When to Request Testimonials

Request a testimonial when:

- A project completed successfully with positive customer feedback
- The customer has indicated willingness to share their experience
- You have documented approval to contact them for marketing use
- The story can be told without confidential project details

Do not request testimonials during active disputes, NDA programs, or unresolved quality issues.

---

## How to Request Approval

1. Open `/admin/testimonials` → **Request Builder** tab
2. Enter customer name, company, email, and project context
3. Click **Generate Request Draft** or **Copy Email**
4. Send the email manually from your business email client
5. Mark **Manually Sent** and later **Testimonial Received**

Email is **not sent automatically** from the website.

---

## How to Record Approval

1. Create a new testimonial in the **Testimonials** tab
2. Enter the approved quote exactly as the customer confirmed
3. Set **Approval received**, **Approval date**, and **Approval method**
4. Configure **Display mode** and approved public display fields
5. Complete the **Approval Checklist** with evidence notes

---

## How to Review Confidentiality

Before publishing:

- Remove confidential project details, pricing, and proprietary information
- Verify no unsupported certification or performance claims
- Confirm display mode matches customer permission
- Mark **Confidentiality review complete**

---

## How to Publish

1. Complete all checklist items (passed)
2. Enable **Approved for public use**
3. Review the **Preview** tab
4. Use **Publishing** tab → **Publish Testimonial**

Publishing is blocked until all requirements are met.

---

## Public Display

- Published testimonials load from Supabase on public pages
- If none are published, representative testimonials show with **Representative Example** label
- If Supabase fails to load, site falls back to representative testimonials
